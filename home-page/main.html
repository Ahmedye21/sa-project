NOVELS

My roommate is vampire

40 LE

You don't find many cheap apartments with a quiet roommate, but mine is quiet, it just he doesn't show up in the mirror either! I hope he's not a vampire.

Match

40 LE

Let's say that opposites are attracted, and kids like dogs. But does having opposite dogs attract humans to each other?

Jane Eyre

40 LE

Jane lived as an outcast to everyone, from her uncle's wife to the school's church priest, but that did not prevent her from applying to work as a teacher to the son of a terrifying man. But did he also ostracize his daughter as Jane was ostracized?!

Sense and Sensibility

60 LE

The novel follows the young ladies to their new home, a meagre cottage on a distant relative's property, where they experience love, romance, and heartbreak.

Pride and Prejudice

70 LE

Sparks fly when spirited Elizabeth Bennet meets single, rich, and proud Mr. Darcy. But Mr. Darcy reluctantly finds himself falling in love with a woman beneath his class. Can each overcome their own pride and prejudice?

The Happiness Hypothesis

40 LE

Author: Jonathan Haidt
The Happiness Hypothesis could be that happiness comes from within, as Buddha said, or could be that happiness comes from outside. Haidt argues in Chapter Six that the truth might lie between the two extremes and contends that love depends on more than the self and is crucial to happiness.

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

40 LE

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck argues that individuals should seek to find meaning through what they find to be important and only engage in values that they can control. Values (such as popularity) that are not under a person's control are, according to the book, "bad values."

Fever Pitch

40 LE

Author: Nicholas Peter John Hornby
"Fever Pitch" is Nick Hornby's account of his life as a football fan. In the book, he talks about how his father took him to his first football match when he was around eleven years old and how by the end of the evening he had fallen in love with the game.

Atomic Habits

60 LE

Author: James Clear
A comprehensive, practical guide on how to change your habits and get 1% better every day.

The Fight

70 LE

Author: Norman Mailer
The presentation and behavior of both Muhammad Ali and George Foreman leading up to "The Rumble in the Jungle."
